Arc Titan




  • GOAL: Medium-close range damage, ability spam, consistent lightning up-time, lots of Ionic Traces.
  • ASPECTS: Touch Of Thunder / Knockout.
  • FRAGMENTS: Spark Of Resistance, Spark Of Shock, Spark Of Magnitude, Spark Of Discharge.
  • EXOTIC GEAR USED: Witherhoard (Kinetic Grenade Launcher) and Heart Of Inmost Light (Chest Armour).
  • OTHER WEAPONS: IKELOS_SMG_v1.0.3 (Arc Submachine Gune - Special Weapon Slot) and Stormchaser (Arc Linear Fusion Rifle - Heavy Weapon Slot).
  • ELEMENTAL WELL MOD DISTRIBUTION: See Table Below.
  • SUPER ABILITY: Thundercrash.
  • SUMMARY: This is easily the easiest and most ability spam build I have. It comes down to a single outlier: Heart Of Inmost Light. I'm pretty sure the exotic chest piece is going to be re-worked or nerfed in some way going into the Lightfall expansion. The chest piece speeds up the cool-down of your 'non-active' ability or abilities. So for example, if you use your grenade, using your melee or class ability will speed up the cool-down of your grenade ability. The reduction of cool-down times is extremely significant so you are always throwing grenades, using your melee, or have your barricade up. I also use this chest piece on my Titan void build. For stat distribution, my only 100 stat is in Resilience for survival. Maxing out grenade, or strength, isn't needed because of how the chest piece works. For weapons, I'm using a crafted IKELOS_SMG with the Voltshot perk for fast mob clears with chain lightning and I also added the Threat Detector perk for fast reload speeds since Voltshot procs off of weapon reloads after kills. I always use Witherhoard when I have an SMG equipped as it not only is great for mob clears, it also helps push the SMG damage that can be lacking at times against harder enemies. Boss damage comes from either the Thundercrash super or from the Stormchaser linear fusion rifle. If you want more boss damage from the super, you can swap out HOIL (Heart Of Inmost Light) and use the Cuirass Of The Falling Star exotic chest armour, but you will be giving up the ability spamming.
