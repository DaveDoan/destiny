Solar Warlock




  • GOAL: Medium-close range damage, team support, lots of scorch stacks.
  • ASPECTS: Heat Rises / Touch Of Flame.
  • FRAGMENTS: Ember Of Singeing, Ember Of Torches, Ember Of Ashes, Ember Of Searing
  • EXOTIC GEAR USED: Witherhoard (Kinetic Grenade Launcher) and Phoenix Protocol (Chest Armour).
  • OTHER WEAPONS: Calus Mini-Tool (Solar Submachine Gun - Special Weapon Slot) and Palmyra-B (Stasis Rocket Launcher - Heavy Weapon Slot).
  • ELEMENTAL WELL MOD DISTRIBUTION: See Table Below.
  • SUPER ABILITY: Well Of Radiance.
  • SUMMARY: First, this is not the Starfire Protocol/Fusion Grenade build that many solar Warlocks run. Second, the first screenshot in the carousel below shows 100 points into Recovery which is incorrect. I have actually redone the build with 100 points in Resilience, 81 in Discipline and 70 into Strength. This build is all about providing support to my fireteam utilizing the Well Of Radiance super, adding lots of scorch stacks and mob clearing with Incandescent on weapons, and making you and your team Radiant by using the Incinerator Snap melee ability to provide some additional damage output. Incandescent on rapid firing weapons, such as an SMG, is fantastic for mob clearing and I use Witherhoard to help push the SMG damage a little more. I use the Palmyra-B rocket launcher with this build for boss damage and additional mob clearing but it's not mandatory. I just like the weapon. The Phoenix Protocol exotic chest armour helps to regen your super when you are standing in the Well Of Radiance but it's also not mandatory. If I'm not concerned with reducing my super cool-down, I'll swap out the chest for the Claws Of Ahamkara exotic gauntlets to have a second melee charge. You could also use the Necrotic Grips exotic gauntlets with the Osteo Striga exotic SMG and have Incandescent on either your energy slot weapon, heavy slot weapon, or both. Survival comes from your healing rift, the Well Of Radiance super, and the Heat Rises aspect that gives you a burst of healing when you consume your grenade.
