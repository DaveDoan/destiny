Solar Hunter




  • GOAL: Medium-close range damage, survival, grenade/melee ability spam.
  • ASPECTS: On Your Mark / Knock 'Em Down.
  • FRAGMENTS: Ember Of Wonder, Ember Of Combustion, Ember Of Singeing, Ember Of Torches, Ember Of Ashes.
  • EXOTIC GEAR USED: Witherhoard (Kinetic Grenade Launcher) and Young Ahamkara's Spine (Gauntlets).
  • OTHER WEAPONS: Calus Mini-Tool (Solar Submachine Gun - Special Weapon Slot) and Cry Mutiny (Solar Grenade Launcher - Heavy Weapon Slot).
  • ELEMENTAL WELL MOD DISTRIBUTION: See Table Below.
  • SUPER ABILITY: Blade Barrage
  • SUMMARY: This build is my second favourite Hunter build and also one of my favourite builds of all my characters. This is one of those builds that synergizes great and everything 'just works'. The two key elements for this build are the Young Ahamkara's Spine gloves and the Knock 'Em Down aspect. The gloves provide near-constant spamming of your Trip Mine grenades and the aspect refunds your melee on final blows while you are radiant. I love the Knife Trick melee so that's what I use. Combine the melee with the Ember Of Torches fragment that makes you radiant with just a melee attack, and you have constant melee spams. Building scorch stacks against targets will cause them to ignite and explode once enough stacks are applied. I build stacks using my melee ability, the Incadescent perk on my SMG and the Ember Of Ashes fragment that applies more scorch stacks to enemies. Becoming radiant, which grants additional damage to you and your fireteam, is achieved by melee attacks and also the Acrobat's Dodge. For stat distribution, I focused on 100 mobilty for the low cool-down on the dodge and 100 into resiliece for survival. I didn't worry about discipline since the Ahamkara gloves regen my grenade almost immediately, and strength wasn't needed for my melee since that takes care of itself with the Knock 'Em Down fragment. If you wanted even more survival options, you can swap out the trip mine grenade for the healing one, but just remember that the benefits of the Ahamkara gloves only applies to trip mine grenades. You can also swap in the Star Eater Scales exotic legs, if you have them, for more damage when you use the blade barrage super. Weapon choices are optional but a solar element weapon(s) with Incandescent synergizes perfectly. I also use Witherhoard to help push that SMG damage a little more in addition to boss damage. As I said, everything 'just works' together in this build. Dodge->Grenade->Melee->Grenade and rinse and repeat while doing weapon damage and watching everything burn.
