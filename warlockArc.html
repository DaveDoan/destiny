Arc Warlock




  • GOAL: Medium-close range damage, fast ability regen, consistent lightning up-time, lots of Ionic Traces.
  • ASPECTS: Arc Soul / Electrostatic Mind.
  • FRAGMENTS: Spark Of Resistance, Spark Of Shock, Spark Of Magnitude, Spark Of Discharge.
  • EXOTIC GEAR USED: Delicate Tomb (Arc Fusion Rifle) and Fallen Sunstar (Helm Armour).
  • OTHER WEAPONS: Stormchaser (Arc Linear Fusion Rifle - Heavy Weapon Slot).
  • ELEMENTAL WELL MOD DISTRIBUTION: See Table Below.
  • SUPER ABILITY: Chaos Reach or Stormtrance.
  • SUMMARY: The two key elements for this Warlock build are the Delicate Tomb fusion rifle and the Fallen Sunstar exotic helmet. Ionic traces provide additional ability cool-down reduction and the fusion rifle creates traces on each kill. Pair that with the helmet's trait that creates additional ability energy from ionic traces and you end up with close to constant ability spamming. You can use whatever weapon of choice for the kinetic slot. I use an SMG for special ammo creation if I find I'm needing more and the Stormchaser linear fusion rifle in the heavy weapon slot provides great boss damage in addition to another option for some mob clearing/major enemy damage. Because of the consistent ability regen, I focused my stats into Recovery to guarantee my healing rift is always up in addition to the Arc Soul (arc buddy). Survival comes from the healing rift and also the Spark of Resistance fragment. Ideally I would like to have dual 100 stats for both resistance and recovery and that will be something I'll be working on once Lightfall is released.
