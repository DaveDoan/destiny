Void Titan




  • GOAL: Medium-close range damage, consistent melee ability up-time.
  • ASPECTS: Offensive Bulwark / Controlled Demolition.
  • FRAGMENTS: Echo Of Instability, Echo Of Leeching, Echo Of Expulsion, Echo Of Undermining.
  • EXOTIC GEAR USED: Witherhoard (Kinetic Grenade Launcher) and Heart Of Inmost Light (Chest Armour).
  • OTHER WEAPONS: Funnelweb (Void Submachine Gun - Special Weapon Slot) and Taipan-4fr (Void Linear Fusion Rifle - Heavy Weapon Slot.
  • ELEMENTAL WELL MOD DISTRIBUTION: See Table Below.
  • SUPER ABILITY: Ward Of Dawn.
  • SUMMARY: When I finally got around to playing my Titan after Void 3.0 launched with Witch Queen, I wanted two things out of my new build. I wanted consistent Volatile Rounds and I wanted to use the new Shield Toss melee ability as much as possible. The best way to achieve both is to use the Heart Of Inmost Light exotic chest armour. I'm pretty sure the chest piece is going to be re-worked or nerfed in some way going into the Lightfall expansion. The chest piece speeds up the cool-down of your 'non-active' ability or abilities. So for example, if you use your grenade, using your melee or class ability will speed up the cool-down of your grenade ability. The reduction of cool-down times is extremely significant so you are always throwing grenades, using your melee, or having your barricade up. I also use this chest piece on my Titan arc build at times. Enemies become volatile from either melee ability damage when using the Controlled Demolition aspect or from grenade kills if you use the Echo Of Instability fragment so you will have Volatile Rounds active quite consistently with whatever void damage weapon you are using. I use the Funnelweb void SMG for fast mob clearing with Volatile Rounds. Survival for you and/or your fireteam comes from 100 points in Resistence, your barricade and also the Ward Of Dawn super. Volatile enemies that explode will also grant you and allies health, as will melee ability final blows if you are using the Echo Of Leeching fragment.
